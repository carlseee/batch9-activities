CRISTIANO RONALDO


A lethal striker born on February 5, 1985 from Portugal quickly became a sensational icon in the football world. Dominating every stat line across the board both internaltionally and for his clubs in Europe. He continues to showcase his talents on and off the pitch chasing for his 6th Ballon d'Or award & a World Cup glory to add to his resume.




FUN FACTS ABOUT CRISTIANO RONALDO

Ronaldo played for 4 clubs in his career: Sporting Lisbon, Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus!

Ronaldo is the only player to win the unique combination of the league title, domestic cup, domestic Supercup, Champions League, Club World Cup, League player of the year, Golden Shoe and Ballon d Or at two clubs (Manchester United & Real Madrid).

ACHIEVEMENTS!

Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has 5 Ballon d'Or trophies to his name. He holds the record for most goals and assists in the UEFA Champions League (134 and 42 respectively), and the record for most goals in the UEFA European Championship (9), its qualification stage (31), and the FIFA Club World Cup (7).

TIMELINE OF CRISTIANO RONALDO

EARLY CAREER

From an early age, he dedicated his life playing football. His talent, enthusiasm and love for the game soon became apparent. By the age of ten, his talent was becoming well known in Portugal and he was signed for Nacional, a Portuguese side from Madeira. He later signed for Sporting Lisbon one of the biggest clubs in Portugal.
His first international exposure came when representing Portugal under 17s at the UEFA championships. His performance caused him to be marked by leading European football managers. In 2003, he played against Manchester United when Sporting beat them 3-1 in a friendly.

MANCHESTER UNITED (2003-2009)

He started playing for Manchester in the 2003/04 season. At Manchester United, his career steadily progressed. In the 2006/07 season, Ronaldo was a key figure in the teams successful league campaign. Ronaldo scored over 20 goals, as he won his first championship. In March 2007, his prowess helped him to gain a record-breaking 120,000 Euros a week five-year contract. He also won PFA player of the year award.
The 2007-08 season was even better, with Cristiano Ronaldo being named the player of the tournament, as Manchester United won the coveted Champions League trophy. The most prestigious club competition in the world.

REAL MADRID (2009-2018)

In 2009, Manchester United accepted an offer of 80 million Euros from Real Madrid, making Ronaldo the most expensive player in the world. Despite intense pressure, Ronaldo thrived in the atmosphere at Real Madrid reaching 100 La Liga goals in just three seasons at Real Madrid. He went on to become the teams top goal scorer of all time and also La Liga second highest goalscorer of all time.
With Ronaldo, Real Madrid have won the La Liga just twice 2011 and 2016. However, he has guided Real Madrid to four UEFA Champions League: 2013, 2015, 2016, 2017.

JUVENTUS (2018-PRESENT)

On 10 July 2018, Ronaldo was signed by Italian club Juventus. In his first season, he helped Juventus to another Serie A title, and Ronaldo became the first player to win national titles in the big three leagues of England, Spain and Italy. However, Ronaldo's eye is still on the prize as he tries to win the Champions League from three different clubs.
